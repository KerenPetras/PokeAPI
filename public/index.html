WHAT IS POKEDEX?

As a Pokemon trainer, one of the most essential tools in your arsenal is the Pokedex.
It's an electronic device that stores information on all the different Pokemon species that you may encounter during your adventures.
The Pokedex provides detailed descriptions of each Pokemon, including its physical characteristics, behavior, habitat, and even its moveset.
It's a valuable resource for any trainer who wants to learn more about the Pokemon they encounter, and it's essential for completing your collection.
With a Pokedex, you'll be able to keep track of all the Pokemon you've caught and those that you still need to capture.
Whether you're a seasoned trainer or a novice just starting, having a Pokedex is an essential tool to becoming a successful Pokemon trainer.
